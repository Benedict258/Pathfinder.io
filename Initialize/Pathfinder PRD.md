## 1. Executive Summary

Pathfinder.io is an AI-powered career guidance and digital skills platform built for students and individuals who want to break into or grow within the technology sector.

The platform was conceived from firsthand experience working at **Nihub**, a digital skills training hub where a recurring pattern was observed: students consistently arrived seeking **direction**, not just resources.

The core insight driving Pathfinder.io is simple: most people trying to enter tech do **not** have a resource problem; they have a **direction problem**.

Existing tools such as roadmap.sh provide comprehensive resource lists, but they offer no personalization and often overwhelm users with five or more learning materials per concept. Pathfinder.io solves this with a different philosophy:

- **One curated path**
- **One focused resource per concept**
- **An AI system that understands the user before offering guidance**

---

## 2. Problem Statement

The following pain points were identified through direct user observation and are the primary motivation for building Pathfinder.io:

- Students from non-technical backgrounds perceive technology as inaccessible and do not know where to begin their learning journey.
- University students in non-engineering programmes leave school without any digital skills, despite operating in a technology-adjacent academic environment.
- Existing roadmap tools list five or more learning resources per concept, causing decision fatigue rather than enabling progress.
- There is no personalized guidance tool that recommends a technology path based on a user's specific field of study, interests, and personal goals.
- Professionals seeking to reskill or upskill lack a structured, Africa-aware platform that speaks to their context.
- Students frequently miss technology opportunities, events, and scholarships due to the absence of a centralized and personalized discovery channel.

---

## 3. Goals and Objectives

### Primary Goals

- Provide **AI-powered path discovery** that helps any user, regardless of academic or professional background, identify the right digital skill for them.
- Deliver **clean, focused learning roadmaps** with one curated resource per concept, removing the overwhelm associated with existing tools.

### Secondary Goals

- Recommend digital skills based on the user's field of study or professional background (for example, recommending data analysis for a Business student, or UX design for a Fine Arts student).
- Surface relevant technology news, scholarship opportunities, internships, and community events.
- Support emerging technologies as standalone, continuously updated learning tracks.
- Enable users to track their learning progress, maintain streaks, and revisit topics over time.

---

## 4. Target Users

Pathfinder.io is designed for anyone seeking to find or grow a path in technology.

The platform's initial focus is the **Nigerian and broader West African market**, with global expansion planned for version 2.

| User Type                  | Profile                                                                    | Primary Need                                                                |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Non-Technology Student** | Studies Law, Medicine, Mass Communication, Business, or Arts               | Find a digital skill that complements their degree and future career        |
| **Technology Student**     | Studies Computer Science, Engineering, or Information Technology           | A clear, focused roadmap with one resource per concept, not a resource dump |
| **Career Switcher**        | A working professional from any background looking to transition into tech | Structured reskilling path, career guidance, and relevant job opportunities |

---

## 5. Product Principles

- **Direction before resources**
- **One path, one concept, one resource**
- **Personalization before recommendation**
- **Clarity over volume**
- **Africa-aware, context-aware guidance**

---

## 6. Scope Notes

Version 1 is a web application. The product is expected to expand later into multilingual support and broader opportunity discovery.

The key design constraint is that Pathfinder.io must not become a resource dump. Every feature should support the goal of helping a user move from confusion to direction.
