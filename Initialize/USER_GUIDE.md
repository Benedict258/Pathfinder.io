# Pathfinder.io — User Guide

## What is Pathfinder.io?

Pathfinder.io is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps you discover which digital/tech skill path to pursue. Instead of overwhelming you with dozens of resources, we give you one clear path with one focused resource per concept.

## How to use Pathfinder.io

### 1. Find Your Path

There are three ways to discover your ideal tech path:

**Chat Onboarding (Recommended)**
Go to `/onboarding` and the Chat tab. Pathfinder's AI will have a short conversation with you — asking about your background, interests, goals, and available time. After 2-4 exchanges, you'll receive personalized path recommendations with confidence scores.

**Form Onboarding**
On the same `/onboarding` page, switch to the Form tab. Fill in your background, select your interests, choose your goal and weekly time commitment. Submit to get instant recommendations.

**Field of Study**
Go to `/field-study` and enter your field of study or profession (e.g., "Business Administration", "Mass Communication"). Pathfinder will recommend tech paths that naturally complement your background.

### 2. Explore Roadmaps

Go to `/roadmaps` to browse all available learning paths. Each roadmap contains 3 focused steps:

- A clear explanation of what you'll learn
- One curated resource to get started
- A "Go Deeper" section with optional extra resources

Available paths:
- Frontend Development
- Data Analysis
- UI/UX Design
- Cybersecurity
- Product Management

### 3. Track Your Progress

Sign in to track your learning progress. On any roadmap, you can mark concepts as "Completed" as you work through them. Pathfinder tracks your streak — how many consecutive days you've made progress — to keep you motivated.

### 4. Discover Opportunities

Visit `/opportunities` to browse curated tech opportunities for the African market:
- Scholarships and training programmes
- Internships and graduate programmes
- Tech conferences and events
- Entry-level job openings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Pathfinder.io free?
Yes. All features are currently free during the beta phase.

### How does the AI work?
Pathfinder uses Llama 3.3, a powerful open-source language model, running on Groq's fast inference platform. The AI analyzes your background, interests, and goals to recommend the best tech path for you.

### What if I don't know my interests yet?
Use the Chat onboarding — the AI will ask you questions to help you discover what excites you about tech. You can also try the Field of Study page to get recommendations based on what you already know.

### Can I change my path later?
Yes. You can revisit onboarding at any time to get fresh recommendations. Your progress is tracked per roadmap, so you can explore multiple paths.

### What happens to my data?
Your onboarding responses, recommendations, and learning progress are stored securely in our database. We do not share your data with third parties.

### Which browsers are supported?
Pathfinder.io works on all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. The site is also fully responsive for mobile use.

### How do the confidence scores work?
Confidence scores (55-95%) reflect how well a path matches your profile. Higher scores mean a stronger fit. You should still read the reasoning and choose the path that excites you most.

### Where are the opportunities sourced from?
Our opportunities feed is curated from trusted programmes across Africa, including Andela, ALX, Flutterwave, Google, and community events like OSCAFest and DevFest.

### How do I track my learning progress?
Sign in, go to any roadmap, and click "Mark complete" on completed concepts. Your streak counts consecutive days of learning activity.

### I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please report issues on our GitHub repository at https://github.com/Benedict258/Pathfinder.io/issues

## Getting the Most from Pathfinder

1. **Start with Chat onboarding** — The conversation helps you discover interests you might not have considered
2. **Try Field of Study matching** — Your academic background is a valuable bridge into tech
3. **Follow one roadmap at a time** — Our philosophy is "one path, one concept, one resource"
4. **Mark progress daily** — Even small steps build momentum and maintain your streak
5. **Check opportunities regularly** — New programmes and events are added frequently
